A Favorite Visualization

Describe the visualization: Exonic deletions in NRXN1 have been associated with several neurodevelopmental disorders, including autism, schizophrenia and developmental delay. However, the molecular mechanism by which NRXN1 deletions impact neurodevelopment remains unclear. Here I used human induced pluripotent stem cells (hiPSCs) as models to investigate the functional impacts of NRXN1 knockdown. Half reduction of NRXN1 expression resulted in changes of expression levels for the cell adhesion pathway (20 genes, P = 2.8*10-6) and neuron differentiation pathway (13 genes, P = 2.1*10-4), implicating that single-gene perturbation can impact gene networks important for neurodevelopment.

Why did you choose it? I choose this as it is the early age of using next generation sequencing technology and induced pluripotent stem cells to analysis the complex gene network and it is my first publication on NGS field.
